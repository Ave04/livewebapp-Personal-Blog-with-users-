About Me

This is what I do.

Hi, I’m Avish (or Ave ;) )! I’m an aeronautical engineering student at Imperial College London, passionate about blending technology, problem-solving, and creativity. My work spans everything from designing control systems for drones to developing components for racing cars, and I’m always looking for ways to merge mechanical engineering with cutting-edge software. I’m also actively learning and developing software, exploring new technologies, and expanding my coding skills in areas like AI, web development, and automation.

When I’m not immersed in engineering or software projects, you’ll find me competing in tennis (I’m a national champion!), or training in Muay Thai, boxing, and football at a competitive level. I also enjoy staying active with volleyball, swimming, and mountain climbing when I get the chance.

Fluent in English, French, and Mauritian Creole, I’m always keen to share what I’m learning, whether it’s about tech, engineering, or my sports experiences. This blog is where I combine my passions for innovation, tech, and an active lifestyle. Thanks for stopping by—I’m excited to share my journey with you!
